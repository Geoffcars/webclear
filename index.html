Vision

To see every bright child in Kenya reach their full potential regardless of their background.

We are dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty by offering underprivileged children access to food, shelter, and education.

About Us

Cornerstone Charity is a non-profit organization focused on supporting the growth, learning, and wellbeing of underprivileged children across Kenya.
